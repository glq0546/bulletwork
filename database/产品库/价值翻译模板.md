# 价值翻译模板

**日期**：2026-06-13

## 核心内容

价值翻译的目标是把「功能型描述」改成「用户结果型描述」。用户不关心你做了什么功能，用户关心自己能省多少钱、省多少时间、少承担什么风险。

## 一、通用公式

```text
把 [用户当前痛苦动作] 变成 [更快/更省/更安全的结果]，帮助你 [省时间/省钱/降低风险]。
```

## 二、功能 → ROI转换表

| 功能型描述 | ROI型描述 |
|---|---|
| AI weekly report generator | 把零散工作记录在约60秒内变成专业英文周报，减少每周重复整理时间 |
| 100+ merchant code dictionary | 快速看懂账单上的乱码商户名，避免误判、漏退或不必要恐慌 |
| 5 dispute letter templates | 直接复制英文争议信，省去不知道怎么和银行沟通的时间 |
| Hidden subscription audit checklist | 系统检查隐藏订阅，减少继续被扣费的风险 |
| Bank phone number list | 遇到扣款问题时不用临时搜索，直接联系正确部门 |
| FAQ Schema/GEO优化 | 让页面更容易被Google和AI搜索引用，积累长期免费流量 |

## 三、BulletWork示例

原始功能描述：`BulletWork is an AI weekly report generator.`

价值翻译后：`BulletWork turns messy work notes into a clean English weekly report in about 60 seconds, so you stop wasting Monday morning formatting status updates.`

更强版本：`If you spend 15 minutes every week writing status updates, BulletWork gives that time back by turning rough notes into a professional report in about a minute.`

## 四、ChargeDecode示例

原始功能描述：`Includes 100+ merchant codes and 5 dispute templates.`

价值翻译后：`Quickly identify confusing credit card charges and use copy-ready templates to take action when a charge looks wrong.`

更强版本：`One unwanted subscription or duplicate charge can cost more than this guide. Use it to understand the charge, contact the right place, and dispute it with a clear written template.`

## 五、Gumroad产品页模板

```text
You found a charge you don't recognize. Now what?

This guide helps you:
- Identify who charged your card
- Understand whether it looks normal or suspicious
- Find the right refund/dispute path
- Copy a ready-to-use dispute letter
- Audit hidden subscriptions that may keep billing you

It is not legal or financial advice. It is a practical toolkit for consumers who want to take the next step without starting from zero.
```

## 六、标题转换模板

Merchant Code Dictionary → Stop Guessing What That Credit Card Charge Is。

Dispute Letter Templates → Copy-Ready Letters for Fighting Unwanted Charges。

Weekly Report Generator → Stop Dreading Monday Morning Status Updates。

Pain Point Report → Weekly User Pain Signals You Can Turn Into Products。

## 七、检查清单

写完任何产品描述后，问五个问题：用户能不能一眼看懂这对他有什么用；有没有说清楚节省时间、节省金钱或降低风险；有没有避免只列功能；有没有具体场景；有没有夸大承诺或合规风险。
