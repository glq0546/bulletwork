# What is SPOTIFY USA on my credit card statement?

                This is a charge from Spotify for a Premium subscription. You signed up for ad-free music and podcast streaming, and it auto-renewed this month.

## Full Name & What It Is

            SPOTIFY USA is the billing code for Spotify's paid subscription. The USA part shows the charge comes from Spotify's American business office, even if you used an app store to sign up. Plans: Individual ($11.99/mo), Duo ($16.99/mo for 2 people), Family ($19.99/mo for up to 6 people), Student ($5.99/mo with verification). Most people see this charge because they signed up for a free trial (1-3 months) and forgot to cancel before it converted to paid. Student plan discounts also expire after 4 years, causing the price to jump to full individual rate without notice.

                Merchant Code: SPOTIFY USA

                Company: Spotify USA, Inc.

                Service: Spotify Premium Subscription

## Common Scenarios

                **1. Free Trial Expired**You signed up for a 3-month free trial during a summer promotion and forgot to cancel before the first real bill hit your account.

                **2. Student Discount Ended**You were on a student plan for four years, but since you graduated, the discount expired and your bill jumped to the full individual price.

                **3. Family Member Used Your Card**Your child or teenager used your credit card to upgrade their personal Spotify account so they could listen to music offline.

                **4. Family Plan Setup**You started a Family plan to share music with your housemates but didn't realize the billing would start as soon as the first month was over.

                **5. Forgotten Subscription**You haven't used the app in months, but the recurring monthly charge keeps coming out because the subscription is still active.

## Is This a Scam?

                ✅ Legitimate charge from Spotify.

                Honestly, most of these are fine. It's a real charge from Spotify for a service you or someone in your house likely signed up for. But if you don't even have a Spotify account, check with your family. It's very common for a spouse or child to link your card without telling you. To verify, check your email for a receipt from Spotify — they send one every month. Also check if you signed up using Facebook or Apple ID, which uses a different email than you expect.

                    Why is Spotify charging me $11.99?
