# What is PAYPAL *NETFLIX on my credit card statement?

                This is your Netflix subscription being billed through PayPal instead of directly from Netflix. You set up Netflix to charge your PayPal account, which then charges your credit card.

## Full Name & What It Is

            PAYPAL *NETFLIX means you're paying for your Netflix membership through PayPal's payment system. Most people are used to seeing NETFLIX.COM on their bank statements. If you see this specific PayPal version, it's because you chose PayPal as your payment method when you signed up. Netflix doesn't charge your card directly — they ask PayPal for the money, and then PayPal takes it from your linked credit card. Netflix plans: Standard with ads ($6.99/mo), Standard ($15.49/mo), Premium ($22.99/mo). It's very common for people to forget they linked their accounts years ago.

                Merchant Code: PAYPAL *NETFLIX

                Company: Netflix (billed through PayPal)

                Service: Netflix Streaming Subscription

## Common Scenarios

                **1. PayPal One-Touch Signup**You used PayPal checkout to start a Netflix subscription and forgot it wouldn't show up as a direct Netflix charge.

                **2. Expired Card Backup**Your credit card on file with Netflix expired, so the system automatically used your backup PayPal account to keep your service active.

                **3. Family Member Reactivation**You shared your PayPal login with a family member who used it to reactivate a Netflix account on their smart TV.

                **4. Third-Party Promo Signup**You signed up for a promotional offer through a third party that required a PayPal connection for future billing.

## Is This a Scam?

                ✅ Usually legitimate.

                Honestly, most of these are fine. It's a standard way for Netflix to handle billing. But it can be a sign of a "zombie" subscription — you think you cancelled Netflix, but the charge keeps hitting your card. This happens because people cancel their account on the Netflix website but forget to stop the "Automatic Payment" agreement inside their PayPal settings. Check both places. If you don't even have a Netflix account, check with family members first.

                **Step 3.** Use the Netflix Help Center live chat to explain that you were charged accidentally and ask for a refund.

## Frequently Asked Questions

                    Why does my Netflix bill say PayPal?
                    This happens because you chose PayPal as your payment method instead of entering your credit card directly on Netflix. PayPal processes the payment and then charges your linked card.

                    How do I stop Netflix from charging my PayPal?
                    Log into PayPal, go to your settings, and find "Automatic Payments." Look for Netflix in the list and select "Cancel" to stop any future charges.

                    Can I get a refund for a PAYPAL *NETFLIX charge?
                    Yes, you can request a refund through Netflix if you haven't used the service during the billing period. If they refuse, contact PayPal support at 1-888-221-1161 to dispute the charge.
