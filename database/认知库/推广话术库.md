# 推广话术库

**日期**：2026-06-13

## 核心内容

本文件保存已验证或已准备使用的推广话术。使用原则：少推销，多求反馈；少链接，多故事；先共鸣，再产品。

## 一、Reddit私信

通用求反馈版：

```text
Hey, saw your post about weekly reports — I used to dread them too.

I ended up building a simple tool that turns messy notes into a professional report in 60 seconds.

Free to try, no signup. Happy to share the link if you want to check it out.
```

信用卡账单场景：

```text
Hey, I saw your post about not recognizing a charge on your card statement.

I'm building a small resource that explains confusing merchant names like DRI*, SQ*, PYPL, and similar codes.

Not selling anything here — just collecting feedback from people who actually deal with this problem. Want me to send it over?
```

## 二、Reddit评论

周报/Scrum抱怨：

```text
The admin overhead is the worst part. I automated my status updates so it takes me about 60 seconds instead of 15 minutes reformatting the same three sentences every week.
```

信用卡不明扣款：

```text
First thing I'd do is check whether the descriptor is a payment processor or a subscription billing name. A lot of confusing charges are not scams, just badly labeled merchants. If you still don't recognize it, contact the merchant first, then your card issuer.
```

DCC隐藏费用：

```text
DCC fees are especially frustrating because the checkout screen often makes the "convenient" option look safer, while the exchange rate is worse. If you were not clearly shown the fee or conversion choice, keep screenshots/receipts and ask your issuer about a dispute.
```

## 三、r/SideProject求助帖

```text
Title: I built a tool to write weekly reports in 2 minutes — looking for honest feedback

Body:
I built this because I hate writing weekly reports.

The tool takes messy notes like:
- fixed login bug
- talked to design
- waiting for API review

And turns them into a clean English weekly update.

I'm not claiming this is a big startup yet. I just want to know if this is useful to other people or only to me.

If you write weekly reports, I'd love honest feedback — especially what feels missing or unnecessary.
```

## 四、Indie Hackers评论

共鸣型：

```text
This resonates deeply. I just launched my first product after years of overthinking. The build was the easy part — managing expectations and getting the first real users has been the hard part.
```

故事型发帖标题：

```text
I built an AI weekly report tool and nobody is using it — help me figure out why
```

帖子结构：我做了什么；为什么做；当前数据（访问少、零付费、渠道被卡）；我试过什么（PH、Reddit、Quora、Dev.to、SEO）；请求社区判断问题在产品、定位还是获客。

## 六、X Build in Public短帖

```text
Day [X] of building in public:

I learned the hard way that building the product is easier than getting the first users.

Product works. Payment works. SEO pages are indexed.

But distribution is still the real problem.
```

```text
Reddit lesson learned:

Do not promote with a new/low-karma account.
Do not switch accounts too often.
Do not post links before trust.

A channel is an asset. Losing it costs more than waiting 7 days.
```

## 八、使用规则

Reddit低Karma账号不主动私信陌生人；不群发；不承诺收益或退款结果；链接只在对方表达兴趣后发送；每条话术都要根据对方原帖改写20%以上。
