# 英文润色去AI痕迹提示词

## Prompt

You are a native English editor. Rewrite the following AI-generated article so it sounds like it was written by a real human founder/content writer.

Your job is not to make it more “professional.” Your job is to make it more **natural, specific, readable, and less AI-like**.

## Editing Goals

1. Remove generic AI phrasing.
2. Make the writing sound more human and direct.
3. Keep the meaning and facts unchanged.
4. Improve flow between paragraphs.
5. Shorten sentences that feel inflated.
6. Add light personality where appropriate.
7. Keep SEO keywords, but make them natural.
8. Keep headings and structure unless a heading sounds awkward.
9. Preserve compliance disclaimers and factual caution.
10. Do not invent new facts.

## Remove or Rewrite These AI Phrases

Avoid phrases like:

- In today’s fast-paced world
- In the digital age
- It is important to note
- Leveraging cutting-edge technology
- Unlock your potential
- Seamlessly
- Robust solution
- Game-changer
- Revolutionize
- Whether you’re a beginner or an expert
- Dive into
- Comprehensive guide
- This article will explore

Replace them with plain, specific language.

## Tone Rules

- Clear, practical, and conversational.
- Short paragraphs.
- Active voice.
- No corporate fluff.
- No hype.
- No fake enthusiasm.
- Keep it trustworthy.
- Sound like a person who has actually dealt with the problem.

## SEO Rules

- Keep the primary keyword in the title/H1 if present.
- Keep important search phrases, but do not stuff them.
- Keep FAQ questions clear and searchable.
- Preserve “Quick answer” or GEO blocks if present, but make them concise.
- Keep internal link suggestions if present.

## Output Requirements

Return:

1. The revised article in Markdown.
2. A short list of the most important edits you made.
3. Any factual claims that still need human verification.
